\section{Claims Migaki Can Defend}

\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=1.5em]
  \item \migaki{} separates AI execution policy from application logic.
  \item \migaki{} represents model/tool/retrieval/context workflows as optimizable execution graphs.
  \item \migaki{} applies optimization passes under explicit cost, latency, quality, and governance constraints.
  \item \migaki{} lowers portable plans into provider-specific APIs through capability-aware backends.
  \item \migaki{} emits evidence traces so optimizations can be audited, replayed, and benchmarked.
\end{itemize}
